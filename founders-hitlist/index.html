💰 High-Spend Churned Families — Re-engage First, Then Pitch FE

🎯 Active Member — FE Pitch (Call Opening)

"Hey [Name], it's Harry from Blues & Greens. How is [son] going?"

[Let her talk. Don't interrupt. 2-3 min minimum.]

"That's amazing. I love hearing that. You were actually one of the first names I thought of when I was thinking about this. We've got something new and I only want to do it with a handful of families."

"Have you got 5 minutes?"
Key: she talks first. Always. The more she shares, the warmer the pitch lands.

💙 The Offer — What FE Is

"So here's what it is. I'm doing a Founder's Experience with a small group of families — maybe 3 or 4. It's different to what you're doing now."

"It starts with a full day IRL — [son] and I, in Melbourne. We do a proper deep dive — identity work, future goals, the stuff that takes months over Zoom to get to. We do it in one day."

"Then it's 90 days intensive — weekly sessions with me directly, not a mentor. I'm personally in it."

"And we cover accommodation — [son] flies down, we cover where he stays."

"The investment is $9,980. That includes everything."
Price last. Always accommodation line second-to-last — it lands hardest. Don't ask "is that okay?" — just pause after the price.

🔄 Re-Engage Script (Churned / High Spender)

"Hey [Name], it's Harry — from Blues & Greens. I've been thinking about [son] actually."

"I know it's been a while. I just wanted to check in — how's he doing?"

[Let her talk — 2 min minimum.]

"Yeah, I hear that. Look — the reason I'm calling is we've been building something I think is actually perfect for a family like yours..."

[→ Bridge into FE offer if she's warm. If not, just check in, end warm, follow up next week.]
Don't push. The goal of call 1 is reconnection. FE pitch on call 2 if she's warm.

💰 FE Lite — Virtual Version ($4,990)

"If the IRL isn't right for you guys right now — travel, school, whatever — we also have a virtual version. Same 90 days, same direct access to me. No IRL day, no travel."

"That one's $4,990."

"Both are capped. Once spots are gone, they're gone."
Offer the Lite when: family is interstate + travel is a real barrier, OR when price hesitation is immediate.

🛡️ Handling: "I need to think about it"

"Of course. What part are you thinking about?"

[Listen. Then isolate one thing.]

"So if [that thing] wasn't an issue — would this feel right for [son]?"

[If yes:] "Okay. Let's solve [that thing]. What would it look like if..."

[If price:] "Here's the way I think about it. You've already invested [amount] in [son]'s development. That's already shown you how much it matters. This is just going further."
